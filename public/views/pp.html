How We Use Cookies

A cookie is a small file that asks permission to be placed on your computer’s hard drive. Once agreed, the file is added, and it helps analyze web traffic or alerts you when you visit a site.

Cookies allow web applications to tailor operations to your needs, likes, and dislikes by remembering your preferences. We use traffic log cookies to identify pages being used, helping us analyze data to improve our website. The data is only used for statistical analysis and then removed from the system.

Cookies help us provide a better website by monitoring useful and less useful pages. They do not give access to your computer or any information beyond what you choose to share. You can choose to accept or decline cookies via your browser settings.
